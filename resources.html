Resources

A collection of valuable links and resources for parents and teachers of young children.

Department of Education and Training Victoria


Services provided by the Department of Education for parents of young children.

Australian Children's Education & Care Quality Authority


The independent national authority that assists governments in administering the National Quality Framework (NQF) for children's education and care.

Raising Children Network


The Australian parenting website: comprehensive, practical, expert child health and parenting information and activities covering children aged 0-15 years.

SunSmart Australia


Mariposa Family Day Care follows the SunSmart program. We help to develop sunsmart behaviour from an early age.

Acknowledgement of Country

Mariposa FDC acknowledges the traditional custodians of Country, the Wurundjeri and Bunurong people of the Kulin Nation and their connections to it's lands, waters, seas, skies and community.

We pay our respects to elders past and present and to all First Nations people today, including their role in the education and care of children.
